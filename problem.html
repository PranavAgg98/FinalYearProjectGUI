001 : Problem Statement

The universe consists of different astronomical objects such as galaxies, stars, asteroids, quasars, planets, moons, etc. We are going to use a dataset of such astronomical bodies and classifying them into stars, quasars and galaxies There has been significant work already done in this field but we aim to take the next step and make aleap in positive direction to create a methodology that withimprove our accuracy significantly.

The Problem

Galaxies, stars and quasars are almost indistinguishable as the feature values associated with every sample are very close. This makes classifying them a real challenge.

Another problem is the amount of inconsistency and presence of missing values in the dataset which can lead to misclassification of objects which in turn decreases the accuracy and consistency of the model.
